About us

21 Extra Goodness sells home-cooked food to the local community and is created to raise awareness on Down syndrome and other special needs, with the vison that all people are valued and treated fairly regardless of disability.

Problems faced

We would like more people to know about us and what we do. Through the use of social media platforms, we managed to gain outreach and awareness.
We hope to obtain additional guidance on how we can continue to leverage these platforms to gain more outreach and awareness admist the ongoing pandemic.
